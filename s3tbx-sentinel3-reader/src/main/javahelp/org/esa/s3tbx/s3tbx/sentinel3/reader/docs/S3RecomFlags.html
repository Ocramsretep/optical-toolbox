Sentinel-3 Recommended Flags

SNAP generates for OLCI Level-2 data a set of recommended flags. Those flags are defined by an group of experts, named the Sentinel-33 Quality Working Group (S3-QWG). This group observes the data and suggests means to ESA/Eumetsat to maintain high quality. It is suggested to consider these flags when working with Sentinel-3 Level-2 data.

For Land and for Water level-2 products different sets of flags have been defined. They combine already the existing LQSF/WQSF flags to a new flag. Where LQSF and WQSF stand for Land/Water Quality and Science Flags. The intention is to ensure good data quality when using/processing the Level-2 data. In SNAP they are displayed as masks in the Product Explorer and in the Mask manager.
